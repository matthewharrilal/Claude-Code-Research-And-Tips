## Executive Summary

This visual audit cataloged EVERY visually distinct component, mechanism, and emergent effect across all 12 DD (Density Dimension) and OD (Organizational Dimension) exploration HTML files. The audit reveals:

1. **Component Inventory:** 18+ distinct component types with 40+ visual variants
2. **Visual Evolution:** Clear progression from DD (density-focused) to OD (organization-focused + enriched)
