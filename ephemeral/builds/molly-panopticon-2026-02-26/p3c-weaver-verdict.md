## Section 0: Experiential Anchor

This page opens with genuine editorial authority -- a dark header that announces its subject with the confidence of a long-form magazine feature, then drops you into warm cream prose that reads like a writer who knows exactly what they want to say and takes their time saying it. The journey through 8 named zones (The Tower Approach, The Cell Interior, The Busy Cell, The Inner Circle, Cells Up Close, The Moat Courtyard, Building Your Own Tower, Departing the Grounds) builds a convincing architectural tour of a real system, with code blocks that feel earned rather than decorative and blockquotes that land as emotional punctuation marks. The page sags in its middle third where consecutive dark code blocks create a documentation trough, and the ending -- while intellectually complete with its synthesis grid and unanswered questions -- never circles back to the emotional register of its opening, leaving the arc front-loaded.

## Section 8: Ship Decision

### IMPROVE

This is Cycle 0 (initial build). The page arrives at **PA-05 3.0/4** with **Tier 5 7/9** -- a strong initial build in APPROACHING territory, bordering COMPOSED. It has a real editorial voice. The two MECHANICAL fixes (S-09 spacing, 768px card stacking) should be applied before the IMPROVE cycle. The STRUCTURAL fix (navigation) can be integrated during the IMPROVE pass. The COMPOSITIONAL fixes (second-half energy, closing resonance) ARE the IMPROVE cycle.
