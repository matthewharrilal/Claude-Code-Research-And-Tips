### Cross-Wave Structural Findings

**1. Exhaustion Criteria Never Verified:** The skill prescribes 3 universal exhaustion conditions before leaving any wave: (1) delta stabilized, (2) research re-read produces nothing new, (3) "infinite time" answer is invisible changes. These are NEVER verified in the notebook. The builder moves to each next wave without documenting exhaustion checks.

**2. Build-Look-Refine Cycle Severely Underexecuted:** Of 38 documented passes, approximately 4-5 contain a complete build-look-refine cycle (build + screenshot + evaluate + refine). ~30 passes are read-only or build-only without the full cycle. The skill describes this cycle as non-negotiable at every pass of every wave.

**3. Primacy Rotation Universally Ignored:** Every wave prescribes a specific file rotation. No wave followed its rotation. Files were batch-read in combined passes rather than given individual primacy turns. This is the single most pervasive deviation.

**4. Definition Journal Almost Entirely Absent:** The skill requires "My current definition of 'good'" at the START and END of each wave. One entry exists (end of Wave 0). 1 of 18 prescribed entries = 5.5% compliance.

**5. Wave Identity Drift:** Waves 4-7 were renamed and their prescribed activities replaced:
- Wave 4 "Synthesis + Provenance" -> "Integration / Architecture"
- Wave 5 "Combination Mastery" -> "Adversarial Examination"
- Wave 6 "Integration" -> "Detail Refinement"
- Wave 7 "Self-Audit Cycle" -> "Grammar + Guardrail Verification"

The builder reinterpreted waves to match what it wanted to do rather than what was prescribed. Activities performed were generally useful but did not match specifications.

**6. Screenshot Protocol Underexecuted:** The skill prescribes screenshots at 1440px, 1024px, and 768px viewports at every pass. The notebook documents full-page screenshots at approximately 3-4 points in the entire build. The 1024px viewport is never mentioned. The 768px viewport was attempted but blocked by the Playwright DPR 0.667 issue.

**7. Quality vs. Quantity Disconnect:** The work that WAS done is generally competent -- 5-layer assessments are substantive when they appear, adversarial tests catch real issues, accessibility audit is thorough, topology color system is a genuine spatial invention. The deficit is in QUANTITY of engagement, not QUALITY of individual passes. The builder produced a competent page in ~38 passes that the skill says should take ~86+ passes. The question is whether the additional 48+ passes would have produced qualitatively different output or just more of the same.
