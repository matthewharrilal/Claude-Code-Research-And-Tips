## CLASSIFICATION NOTES

1. **Items appearing in multiple extraction files** with the same content (e.g., tokens.css 183 lines appears in d01-d03, d04-d05, and d15-d17) are classified at each occurrence since they carry different item IDs and may have different line references.

2. **Experiment protocol items (Diagram 9)** are classified as META when they describe how to validate the pipeline (not pipeline execution itself), and as L9 ORCHESTRATION when they define gate criteria or pipeline flow.

3. **Gas Town gap analysis items (Diagram 8)** are classified as L2 PERCEPTION when they describe perceptual quality dimensions, and as L5 VALUES when they describe quality tier scores.

4. **Adversarial review items (Diagram 24)** are mostly META as they critique the pipeline design. Individual items are tagged with secondary layers based on which pipeline layer they critique.

5. **Grand synthesis items (Diagram 25)** are META as they classify claims about the pipeline. Secondary layers indicate which layer the claim applies to.

6. **Cross-page coherence items (Diagram 17)** span L1 IDENTITY (soul constraints), L3 SCAFFOLDING (CSS properties), and META (architectural verdicts about coherence mechanism).

7. **Total count reconciliation:** The 885 items are distributed across the 10 categories. Some items had ambiguous primary classification — in those cases, the PRIMARY layer was chosen based on the item's most actionable implication, with the alternative noted in Secondary Layers.
