# Verification: DD/OD/AD/CD Metacognitive Evaluation in /falls-in-love

**Verification question:** Is the metacognitive evaluation COMPREHENSIVELY woven through DD, OD, AD, and CD case study absorption?

**Verdict: NO. There are significant structural gaps.** The skill has a superb metacognitive backbone (the 5 Layers, the Deepening Test, staleness detectors, false convergence signals) and a well-structured Wave 3 with pass-level deepening. But the metacognitive evaluation during case study absorption is DIMENSION-GENERIC, not dimension-specific. The agent is told WHAT to extract from each dimension category (lines 507-510) but is NOT given dimension-specific metacognitive questions to ask WHILE absorbing. The 5-Layer evaluation at Wave 3 (lines 528-533) mentions DD and OD by name in examples but does not prescribe distinct evaluation protocols for each dimension's absorption passes.

Currently the agent is told to "derive from R4" but given no process for doing so. The process should mirror what case studies provide: tension derivation, collapse moment, reusable vs. metaphor-specific separation.

**Location for fix:** Two locations:
1. After line 510, in the new dimension-specific metacognitive questions section
2. After line 495, expand the AD guidance from 2 sentences to a mini-protocol (8-12 lines) explaining HOW to derive axis patterns from R4

## 5. Cross-Dimension Evaluation (Does It EVOLVE?)

### What exists (PARTIAL)

The skill has some cross-dimension evolution:

- **Wave 3 Pass 10-12** (line 539): "Where do DD + OD effects interact?" -- begins compound thinking
- **Wave 4 L5** (line 557): "Did my Wave 2 evaluation miss density-organization interactions?" -- retrospective cross-dimension
- **Wave 5 L1** (line 577): "Do density, organization, and axis operate simultaneously?" -- full compound
- **Wave 5 L2** (line 578): "I saw each mechanism dimension separately... Now CD-006 teaches COMBINATION"

### What is MISSING

**The evolution is IMPLICIT, not EXPLICIT.** The skill does not say: "When you finish DD absorption and begin OD absorption, your evaluation of organizational structure should now INCORPORATE density thinking. You should ask: 'Does the organizational hierarchy interact with the density rhythm? Does the information hierarchy CREATE density variation?'"

The cross-dimension evolution happens naturally at Wave 5, but during Wave 3 the agent absorbs DD, then OD, then CD in sequence (per the rotation schedule) without explicit metacognitive prompts to ACCUMULATE dimensional awareness.

**Missing cross-dimension evaluation prompts:**

After DD absorption, entering OD:
- "Now that I understand the density landscape of this page, does the organizational structure SERVE or FIGHT the density rhythm?"
- "Does the information hierarchy create natural density variation, or do I need to impose density independently of organization?"

After DD+OD absorption, entering CD:
- "I now have density and organization as lenses. Where do they INTERACT on this page? Where do they operate in isolation? The CD case studies will teach me how to COMPOUND them -- but first I need to see where they currently meet."

After DD+OD+CD absorption, applying axis patterns:
- "With density, organization, and compound effects in place, do the reading paths (axis patterns) SERVE or DISRUPT these effects? Does the Z-pattern guide the eye through the density rhythm, or across it?"

**Location for fix:** Within the Wave 3 pass-level deepening (lines 536-541), add explicit cross-dimension metacognitive prompts at the transition points between dimension absorption.

## 6. Per-Pass Evaluation Deepening Within Each Dimension

### What exists (GENERIC)

The skill has excellent per-pass deepening for Wave 3 generally (lines 536-541):
- Passes 1-3: EXTRACTION
- Passes 4-6: TRANSLATION
- Passes 7-9: CONTENT-DEEPENING
- Passes 10-12: COMPOUND VISION
- Passes 13+: DEPTH

### What is MISSING

**The per-pass deepening is DIMENSION-AGNOSTIC.** Pass 1 for DD-006 gets the same guidance as Pass 1 for OD-004 or CD-001. But the cognitive work is fundamentally different:

- Pass 1 absorbing DD-006 should ask: "What density forms exist in this exploration? How many scales? What is the island-to-ocean ratio?"
- Pass 1 absorbing OD-004 should ask: "What organizational logic governs this exploration? What is the confidence gradient? Where is the culmination?"
- Pass 1 absorbing CD-001 should ask: "What dimensions are operating simultaneously? Where is the compound emergence?"

These are categorically different questions requiring categorically different cognitive work. The generic "What spatial ideas does this case study offer?" (line 536) does not distinguish them.

**Furthermore:** Within DD absorption specifically, the evaluation should deepen across passes:
- DD Pass 1: "What density patterns exist?" (perception)
- DD Pass 3: "Which density patterns serve THIS content?" (relevance)
- DD Pass 5: "Does the density at the character level encode semantic meaning from THIS content?" (depth)
- DD Pass 8: "Have I internalized density thinking so deeply that I see density implications in every section without consulting DD case studies?" (internalization)

This per-dimension deepening across passes does not exist. The closest is line 533's Wave 3 L5, which shows density evaluation deepening from Wave 2 to Wave 3 -- but this is across WAVES, not across passes within a DD absorption sequence.

**Location for fix:** Replace or augment lines 536-541 with dimension-aware pass-level deepening. When DD case studies are at primacy, the pass questions should be DD-specific. When OD case studies are at primacy, OD-specific. When CD case studies are at primacy, CD-specific.

### Fix 4: Dimension-Aware Per-Pass Deepening

**Location:** Augment lines 536-541 with dimension-specific pass deepening

This is the most significant structural change. The current generic 5-phase pass structure (EXTRACTION -> TRANSLATION -> CONTENT-DEEPENING -> COMPOUND VISION -> DEPTH) should be SUPPLEMENTED with dimension-specific deepening:

```markdown
**Per-dimension pass deepening (supplements the generic pass structure above):**

DD absorption passes deepen through:
- Early passes: "What density patterns exist in this case study?" (perception)
- Mid passes: "Which density patterns serve THIS content's specific features?" (relevance filtering)
- Late passes: "Does the density at the character level encode semantic meaning from THIS content?" (micro-scale depth)
- Final passes: "Have I internalized density thinking so deeply that I see density implications without consulting DD case studies?" (internalization test)

OD absorption passes deepen through:
- Early passes: "What organizational logic governs this case study? What is its culmination signal?" (perception)
- Mid passes: "Does THIS content's structure share organizational logic with the case study, or does it need a different logic?" (relevance filtering)
- Late passes: "Does the organizational structure I have built make the content's argument VISIBLE in the page's spatial hierarchy?" (argument-as-hierarchy)
- Final passes: "Have I internalized organizational thinking so deeply that I evaluate every zone's hierarchy against the content's meaning?" (internalization test)

AD derivation passes deepen through:
- Early passes: "Which of the 5 axis patterns could serve this content? Which are irrelevant?" (scanning)
- Mid passes: "For the 2-3 candidate patterns, which resolves more design problems simultaneously?" (collapse moment search)
- Late passes: "Does the chosen reading path CREATE meaning the content lacks in prose?" (axis as content invention)
- Final passes: "Is the reading path so content-derived that changing the content would require changing the path?" (coupling test)

CD absorption passes deepen through:
- Early passes: "What dimensions are operating simultaneously in this case study? Where is compound emergence?" (perception)
- Mid passes: "Where on MY page could compound emergence happen? Where are dimensions currently layered but not compounded?" (opportunity identification)
- Late passes: "Can I point to a specific CSS moment where removing either dimension destroys the compound effect?" (compound verification)
- Final passes: "Does the page feel like a SYSTEM where dimensions are inseparable, or a LAYER CAKE where they stack?" (system-feel test)
```

## Overall Assessment

The /falls-in-love skill has a world-class metacognitive backbone. The 5 Layers, the Deepening Test, the staleness detectors, the false convergence signals, the per-wave 5-layer evaluations, and the pass-level deepening within each wave are all excellent. The architecture is right.

But the metacognitive evaluation during DD, OD, AD, and CD case study absorption is **dimension-generic when it needs to be dimension-specific**. The agent is told WHAT to extract from each dimension but not given dimension-specific METACOGNITIVE QUESTIONS to ask during absorption. The generic "What spatial ideas does this case study offer?" applies equally to density, organization, axis, and compound work -- but these are fundamentally different cognitive tasks requiring fundamentally different self-evaluation.

The four fixes above would close this gap without restructuring the skill's architecture. They add ~80-100 lines to a 918-line file (roughly 10% growth), which is proportionate to the significance of the gap.

**Priority order:**
1. Fix 1 (dimension-specific metacognitive questions) -- HIGHEST priority, closes the primary gap
2. Fix 2 (AD derivation mini-protocol) -- HIGH priority, AD is the weakest dimension
3. Fix 3 (cross-dimension evolution prompts) -- MEDIUM priority, makes implicit evolution explicit
4. Fix 4 (dimension-aware per-pass deepening) -- MEDIUM priority, deepens the pass structure
