## 1. Executive Summary

Knowledge propagation across DD -> OD -> AD -> CD follows a clear pattern: **deep theoretical propagation in DD -> OD -> AD, followed by a sharp compression at the AD -> CD boundary.** The first three stages (DD, OD, AD) show genuine intellectual evolution -- findings are not just cited but ELEVATED (DD-F-012's "serves" becomes "IS" in AD-F-004). The CD stage shows citation-level absorption (findings referenced in headers and comments) but not theoretical-level absorption (findings not evaluated, extended, or challenged).

The model is neither purely CUMULATIVE nor purely ADDITIVE. It is **cumulative with a compression cliff at CD**. DD -> OD -> AD is a genuine accumulation where each stage inherits, extends, and deepens prior knowledge. CD is an additive layer that operates on compressed summaries of that accumulated knowledge rather than on the knowledge itself.

**Key metric:** DD-F-006 (fractal) penetrates all 4 stages with full structural compliance. DD-F-012 (density implies axis) is ELEVATED through OD and AD but reduced to implicit compliance in CD. OD-F-005 (org IS density) is the crown jewel of OD, elevated to AD-F-023 (ATTENTION TOPOLOGY) in AD, then flattened to a header declaration in CD. The theoretical arc rises for 3 stages, then plateaus.

**Verified reference counts in CD HTML files:**
- AD-F-023 / ATTENTION TOPOLOGY: 14 occurrences across 5 of 6 files
- DD-F-006 / fractal: 16 occurrences across 3 of 6 files
- OD-F-005 / org IS density: 10 occurrences across 5 of 6 files

## 7. Cross-Tracer Synthesis

### 7.1 The Depth Gradient

All four tracers show the same pattern:

```
DD ----[deep]----> OD ----[deep]----> AD ----[shallow]----> CD
```

The depth gradient is NOT uniform across stages. It drops sharply at the AD -> CD boundary:

| Transition | Average Engagement Level | Pattern |
|-----------|------------------------|---------|
| DD -> OD | Level 2-3 (extension) | Findings extended with new mechanisms |
| OD -> AD | Level 3-4 (elevation) | Findings elevated, formalized, bounded |
| AD -> CD | Level 0-1 (compliance/declaration) | Findings cited in headers, checked binarily |

**One exception:** Tracer 3 (transition grammar) achieves Level 2-3 in CD because the transition grammar is inherently binary/operational -- it survived compression.

### 7.2 Why the Cliff Exists

The mechanism-tracer's analysis (debrief-mechanism-trace.md) reveals the structural cause: CD builders received a **5-layer mediation funnel** that compressed ~30,000 lines of accumulated research into ~250-line per-builder research packages. The compression ratio was 60:1 to 100:1.

This is the architectural answer to why depth collapses at CD:

**DD -> OD -> AD:** Each stage's builders read the PREVIOUS stage's outbound findings directly. OD builders read DD-outbound-findings.md (233 lines). AD builders read OD-outbound-findings.md (960 lines) and DD-outbound-findings.md. The findings themselves carried theoretical depth because they were WRITTEN to carry theoretical depth.

**AD -> CD:** CD builders did NOT read prior outbound findings directly. They received research packages created by Phase 0 agents. The packages compressed 84 prior findings into ~250 lines of per-builder instructions. The compression was necessary (no builder can absorb 30,000 lines), but it stripped theoretical context.

The **compression format** matters enormously:
- OD-outbound-findings.md describes DD-F-012 as: "Density patterns IMPLY axis preferences. Axis geometry must SERVE the density pattern, not fight it."
- AD-outbound-findings.md describes DD-F-012 as: "ELEVATED from 'serves' to 'IS.'" -- the theoretical evolution is captured.
- CD research packages describe DD-F-012 as: part of the "Common References" list with a file path. The evolution is not captured. The theoretical depth is lost.

### 7.3 Is Cumulative or Additive the Right Model?

**Neither. The model is "cumulative with a compression cliff."**

**Stages 2-4 (DD -> OD -> AD) are genuinely CUMULATIVE:**
- Each stage inherits ALL prior findings
- Findings are not just cited but evaluated and often elevated
- The intellectual chain visibly deepens: "implies" -> "serves" -> "IS"
- New mechanisms are discovered that extend prior concepts
- Crown jewels build on prior crown jewels (DD-F-006 -> OD-F-005 -> AD-F-023)

**Stage 5 (CD) is ADDITIVE with respect to practical execution:**
- CD adds new practical knowledge (velocity interleaving, bridge transitions, component chemistry)
- CD does NOT deepen the theoretical chain
- CD's findings are STRUCTURAL (how things compose) not THEORETICAL (why composition works)
- The 25 CD-F findings are observations about successful combinations, not evaluations of whether the theoretical framework predicts those successes

**The compression cliff separates the cumulative regime (DD-OD-AD) from the additive regime (CD).**

### 7.4 The Transition Grammar Exception

Tracer 3 (OD-F-007 -> AD-F-025 -> CD-F-025) shows that propagation CAN work at CD when:
1. The finding has an operational, binary-checkable structure (transition types: Smooth/Bridge/Breathing)
2. CD builders encounter the finding's effects directly (they have to transition between patterns)
3. The finding generates visible, testable artifacts (gap sizes, border elements)

The transition grammar works because it survived the compression by being inherently compressible -- it IS a matrix, a lookup table, a binary classification. Findings that require theoretical understanding (like AD-F-023 ATTENTION TOPOLOGY) cannot survive 100:1 compression.

### 7.5 The Binary Rule Principle in Action

The project's own metacognition analysis identified: "Binary rules achieve 100% agent compliance; judgment rules achieve ~0%." The depth analysis confirms this precisely:

| Finding Type | DD->OD | OD->AD | AD->CD |
|-------------|--------|--------|--------|
| Binary rules (fractal: 5 scales PASS/FAIL) | 100% | 100% | 100% |
| Formal systems (transition grammar: 3 types) | -- | 100% | ~80% (applied + extended) |
| Theoretical claims (density IS axis) | Extension | Elevation | ~0% engagement |
| Meta-concepts (ATTENTION TOPOLOGY) | -- | -- | ~0% engagement |

**The depth of propagation is inversely proportional to the abstractness of the finding.** Concrete, binary-checkable findings propagate perfectly. Abstract theoretical findings propagate only when the receiving agent reads the source document directly (not through a compressed intermediary).

## 8. The 4-Stage Knowledge Lifecycle

Based on the tracer analysis, findings follow a lifecycle:

### Phase A: Discovery (DD)
- Findings emerge from empirical exploration
- They are specific observations ("fractal self-similarity scored highest")
- Format: observation + evidence + implication

### Phase B: Extension (OD)
- Prior findings are consumed and extended with new mechanisms
- Theoretical depth increases ("organization IS density" elevates DD-F-010)
- Format: prior finding + new evidence + deeper implication

### Phase C: Formalization (AD)
- Extended findings are formalized into systems (transition grammar, equivalence mechanisms)
- The intellectual chain reaches its apex (ATTENTION TOPOLOGY)
- Format: formal system + proofs + bounded model

### Phase D: Application (CD)
- Formal systems are applied to new contexts (combinations)
- Binary/operational aspects propagate; theoretical aspects do not
- Format: compliance check + operational findings

**The lifecycle is a rising curve (A -> B -> C) followed by a plateau (D).** Theoretical depth increases through Phase C, then stops. Practical breadth increases in Phase D, but at the cost of theoretical depth.

## 10. Assessment: Is the Knowledge Pipeline Healthy?

### What Works
1. **Binary/operational propagation is perfect.** Soul compliance (border-radius: 0, box-shadow: none) propagates with 100% fidelity across all 4 stages. No exceptions.
2. **Formal system propagation is strong.** The transition grammar (AD-F-025) successfully propagated to CD and was even extended (CD-F-025).

4. **Convention-as-vehicle works.** The convention spec chain (OD-CONV -> AD-CONV -> CD-CONV) successfully propagates structural rules.

### What Doesn't Work
1. **Theoretical findings die at compression.** Any finding whose value is in its REASONING (not just its conclusion) fails to propagate through compressed intermediaries.
2. **Crown jewels are the most vulnerable.** AD-F-023 (the single most important finding) had the weakest theoretical engagement in CD despite being referenced 14 times. References without engagement is citation without understanding.
3. **Evaluation-level engagement is absent at CD.** CD builders APPLIED findings but never EVALUATED them. The R-5 evaluation matrix (a 707-line document with 4 outcome categories: VALIDATED/EXTENDED/CHALLENGED/DEFERRED) was never used.
4. **The audit measured the wrong layer for CD.** Checking "does border-radius equal 0?" is correct for soul compliance but irrelevant to "does ATTENTION TOPOLOGY hold under combination?"
5. **Reference density is uneven.** DD-F-006 appears in only 3 of 6 CD files. AD-F-023 appears in 5 of 6. This suggests findings propagate to files where the builder encountered them in their research package, not uniformly.

### Health Verdict

**The pipeline is healthy for WHAT-level propagation (rules, tokens, binary checks) and sick for WHY-level propagation (theoretical depth, meta-concepts, evaluative reasoning).**

For Migration, this means:
- The design system's RULES will propagate perfectly (tokens, soul, convention)
- The design system's REASONING will not propagate unless explicitly addressed (agents will apply patterns without understanding why they work)
- The "freeze guarantee" is operationally valid (patterns work) but theoretically ungrounded (no proof that patterns are COMPLETE)
- The transition grammar will propagate well (it is operational and was extended by CD)
- ATTENTION TOPOLOGY will not propagate unless Migration agents read AD-outbound-findings.md directly

## 11. The Compression Cliff: A Structural Explanation

The cliff exists because of a fundamental tension in the pipeline's architecture:

**Accumulation requires reading.** For a stage to deeply engage with prior work, its builders must READ the prior outbound findings. DD has 233 lines of outbound findings. OD has 960 lines. AD has 681 lines. The accumulated total entering CD is ~1,874 lines of outbound findings alone, plus convention specs (~2,746 lines combined), plus accumulated identity (~519 lines), plus 39 R-5 findings (~756 lines). Total mandatory reading: ~5,895+ lines.

**Agents have finite context.** A builder agent producing a ~1,700-line HTML file with ~250 lines of research package context is already near its effective working limit. Adding 5,895 lines of mandatory reading would leave no context for the actual building task.

**The compression was necessary.** Phase 0 agents compressed the 5,895+ lines into ~250-line packages. This made building possible but stripped theoretical depth.

**The alternative would be a dedicated theoretical-evaluation phase** -- agents who read the full prior corpus and evaluate whether CD's outputs answer the prior stages' questions. This would be separate from building and auditing. It would require agents with full context of the theoretical chain, not compressed packages.

This is precisely what the intention-analyst's report recommends as a "Phase 4." The depth analysis confirms this is the right intervention -- the cliff is structural, not accidental, and cannot be fixed by changing builder instructions.

## 12. Recommendations

1. **Accept the compression cliff as architectural.** It is a consequence of the binary-rule architecture, which achieves 100% compliance at the cost of theoretical depth. This is a trade-off, not a bug.

2. **Add a theoretical evaluation layer.** After CD building and CSS auditing, run a separate theoretical audit where agents read the full outbound findings chain (DD -> OD -> AD -> CD) and evaluate whether CD answered the questions prior stages posed.

3. **Produce the R-5 evaluation record.** The 707-line R5-EVALUATION-MATRIX.md was never used. Running it would produce the formal VALIDATED/EXTENDED/CHALLENGED/DEFERRED classification that CD was supposed to deliver.

4. **Test AD-F-023 at compound scale.** Take CD-006 (the pilot migration with 7 sections) and ask: does the compound attention topology create a coherent reading experience? This requires perceptual judgment, not binary checking.

5. **Acknowledge the recency gradient.** CD files reference AD (immediate predecessor) far more than DD (earliest predecessor). This is natural but means DD's deepest insights (fractal, density tolerance) need explicit reinforcement in migration instructions.
