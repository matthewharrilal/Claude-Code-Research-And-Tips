# Enrichment Plan: Reframing Documentation for Gallery-Not-Cookbook Access

**Date:** 2026-02-14
**Planner:** rigidity-mechanics team enrichment planner
**Constraint:** Reduce gravitational pull WITHOUT reducing insight
**Core Principle:** "Here's exactly what we tested and what happened" (gallery) not "Here's what works and when to use it" (cookbook)

---

## EXECUTIVE SUMMARY

The Gravity Paradox: **The most useful documents are the most gravitational.** The tension-composition skill works BECAUSE it's precise (839 lines, exact thresholds). The strategy library is valuable BECAUSE it categorizes (4 tiers, 6 recipes). We cannot make these less precise without destroying their utility.

**The solution: SAME precision, DIFFERENT framing.**

### The Two Critical Findings

1. **Timing and framing are deterministic** (cognitive mechanics research)
   - "When to use" triggers lookup mode ‚Üí pattern-matching
   - "Here's proof" triggers derivation mode ‚Üí fresh generation
   - SAME information, opposite creative outcomes

2. **Gravity cliff at pattern layer** (spectrum analysis)
   - Tokens/components/mechanisms = vocabulary (0-50% constraint)
   - Patterns/compositions = decisions (75-100% constraint)
   - The extractable design system is vocabulary; the gravitational danger is at the pattern layer

### The Anti-Gravity Intervention (6 parts)

1. **Timing Control:** Library consulted AFTER metaphor derivation (binary sequential rules)
2. **Framing Rewrite:** "Tension that produced this" not "When to use"
3. **Separation:** Mechanisms (reusable) split from case studies (inspirational)
4. **Explicit Divergence Language:** "You are EXPECTED to derive novel metaphor"
5. **Metaphor Commitment:** Lock-in BEFORE library access
6. **Diversity Tracking:** Make repetition visible, not invisible anchor

### Three Files Need Complete Reframing

1. **08-COMPOSITIONAL-STRATEGY-LIBRARY.md** (CRITICAL gravity ‚Äî 1,747 lines)
2. **05-library-architecture.md** (CRITICAL gravity ‚Äî 40-file spec designed before contents)
