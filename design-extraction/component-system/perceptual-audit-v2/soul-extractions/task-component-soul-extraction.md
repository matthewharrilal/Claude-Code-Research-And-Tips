## Master Perceptual Truth

## Character Guidance

**The Diligent Scribe** marks progress without ceremony. Completion is satisfying but not celebratory.

**Design Mantras:**
- "Mark it done and move on."
- "Progress is visible, not celebrated."
- "The work list, not the achievement board."

**Anti-pattern:** Confetti animations, green checkmarks, progress bars, gamified celebration.

---

## KortAI Alignment

**Level:** STRONG (editorial checklist feel)
