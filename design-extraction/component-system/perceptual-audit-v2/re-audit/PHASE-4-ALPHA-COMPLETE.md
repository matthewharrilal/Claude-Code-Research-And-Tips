### Universal KortAI Structural DNA (All 11 Components)

1. **Sharp corners** (0px radius) — Editorial precision
2. **Flat design** (no shadows, 1px borders max) — Print heritage
3. **Warm cream backgrounds** — Quality paper feel
4. **Whitespace zone separation** — Implicit > explicit
5. **Typography hierarchy** — Size/position, not weight stacking
6. **Red as primary accent** — Consistent semantic use
