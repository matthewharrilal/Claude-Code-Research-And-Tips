## TYPOGRAPHY

### Banner Text ("NOUS HERMES 2")
- **Style**: Bold condensed sans-serif
- **Weight**: 800-900 (Extra Bold/Black)
- **Case**: ALL CAPS
- **Treatment**: Flat white fill, slight shadow/3D effect
- **Letter-spacing**: Tight (-0.02em)
- **Font Match**: Similar to Barlow Condensed Black or Impact
- **Size**: ~8-10% of image height

### Display Text ("MIXTRAL 8X7B")
- **Style**: Chrome/beveled 3D effect
- **Weight**: 900 (Black)
- **Case**: ALL CAPS
- **Treatment**:
  - Multi-layer 3D extrusion
  - Horizontal halftone line shading for depth
  - Gradient from light (top-left) to dark (bottom-right)
  - Each letter has individual depth
- **Letter-spacing**: Normal to slightly tight
- **Font Match**: Wide condensed geometric sans (similar to Bebas Neue but wider)
- **Size**: ~25-30% of image height (massive)

### Body Text ("the new flagship trained...")
- **Style**: Light sans-serif
- **Weight**: 300-400 (Light/Regular)
- **Case**: Sentence case
- **Size**: Small (~2% of image height)
- **Color**: White
- **Alignment**: Left-aligned
- **Font Match**: Similar to Roboto Light or Inter Light

### "nous research" attribution
- **Style**: Monospace or light sans
- **Weight**: 300
- **Case**: lowercase
- **Color**: White

## IMAGERY

### Manga/Anime Illustration
- **Style**: Detailed manga linework
- **Subject**: Muscular male figure, heroic pose
- **Treatment**:
  - Blue halftone shading (dots visible)
  - White highlights for form definition
  - High contrast between light and shadow
  - Dynamic hair/movement lines
- **Mood**: Powerful, heroic, action-oriented

### Halftone Pattern
- **Dot Size**: Medium (~3-4px equivalent)
- **Density**: Variable (denser in shadows)
- **Pattern**: Regular grid, circular dots
- **Color**: Lighter blue on dark blue background
- **Effect**: Creates print/vintage feel

## TEXTURE

### Halftone Application
- Applied to main illustration
- Creates retro print aesthetic
- Blue dots on blue background (tonal variation)
- Dot pattern visible at medium zoom

### Color Gradation
- Achieved through halftone density, not smooth gradients
- Creates stepped tonal transitions
- Evokes CMYK printing/vintage print media

### Grain/Noise
- Subtle paper texture possible
- Adds warmth and vintage feel
