Experiment 1C — Visible Process

Stipple Pattern (Irregular Dots)

Organic, non-grid dot distribution like hand-drawn stippling. The dots cluster organically rather than sitting on a perfect grid. This tests whether "handmade" feeling strengthens or weakens the editorial identity.

PERCEPTUAL QUESTION: Does the irregularity feel INTENTIONAL (hand-drawn art) or BROKEN (technical failure)? Does it evoke pen-and-ink illustration, or does it just look like damaged halftone?
