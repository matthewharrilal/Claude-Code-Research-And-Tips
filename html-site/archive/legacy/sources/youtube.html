Quality Filters

Prioritize Videos That:

  • Have >1000 views (minimum engagement)
  • Are from 2025 or later (post-launch)
  • Include actual demonstrations
  • Show real code and workflows
  • Are >10 minutes for tutorials (depth)
  • Have positive like ratios (>95%)

Avoid Videos That:

  • Are purely promotional without substance
  • Use clickbait titles without delivering
  • Are outdated (pre-launch speculation)
  • Are AI-generated content farms
  • Have disabled comments
