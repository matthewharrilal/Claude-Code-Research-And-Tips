You Are Here: The Philosophy section explores the foundational thinking behind Claude Code practices. Not "how to" but "why to" - the mental models, worldviews, and principles that inform effective agent engineering.

When to Use: When you want to understand the reasoning behind patterns, not just copy them. When you need to adapt practices to new situations not covered elsewhere.

Philosophy

The fundamental thinking that shapes how the best practitioners approach Claude Code. Mental models over memorized procedures.

"CLAUDE.md works so well because it externalizes knowledge that would otherwise get lost between context windows. It's not about optimizing prompts - it's about building persistent institutional memory."
- Boris Cherny, Claude Code Creator

Why Philosophy Matters

Patterns change. Tools evolve. Models improve. But the underlying principles - why certain approaches work and others don't - remain stable.

Mastering philosophy means you can derive new patterns when you encounter situations nobody has documented yet.
