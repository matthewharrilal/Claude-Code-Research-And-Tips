Chapter Essence

The shift from interactive to autonomous development isn't about working lessâ€”it's about working differently. You learn to think in terms of specifications, not conversations. Instead of telling Claude what to do step-by-step, you define the outcome and let it find the path.

The Ralph Wiggum loop (while :; do cat PROMPT.md | claude; done) isn't just a bash trickâ€”it's a philosophy: fresh context beats extended sessions. Every iteration starts clean, reads the truth from files, and produces verifiable output.
