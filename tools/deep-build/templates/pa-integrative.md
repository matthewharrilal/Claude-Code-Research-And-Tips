## YOUR SYNTHESIS

Write your report in these sections:

### 1. CONVERGENT FINDINGS

What do 3+ auditors agree on? List each convergent finding with:
- The finding itself
- Which auditors flagged it (by letter)
- Your own assessment: do you agree with the convergence, or is it groupthink?

### 2. DIVERGENT FINDINGS

Where does 1-2 auditors disagree with the majority? For each divergence:
- What the majority says
- What the dissenting auditor(s) say
- Your assessment: is the dissenter seeing something the majority missed, or are they wrong?

### 3. BLIND SPOTS

What does NO auditor mention that you noticed in your own experiential pass? These are the most valuable findings because they indicate systematic blind spots in the auditor assignment structure.

For each blind spot:
- What you noticed
- Why you think all 8 auditors missed it
- How significant it is

### 4. CROSS-CUTTING PATTERNS

Patterns visible only when multiple auditor perspectives are combined:
- Does the page work differently at desktop vs mobile? (Cross-viewport pattern)
- Do typography findings and spacing findings point to the same root issue?
- Does a strength in one dimension compensate for a weakness in another?

### 5. THE ONE THING

If you could tell the Weaver exactly ONE thing that the 8 individual reports collectively fail to convey, what would it be? This is your unique contribution — the finding that requires holding all perspectives simultaneously.
